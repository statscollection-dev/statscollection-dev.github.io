statscollection.online.classes.Mean¶

class statscollection.online.classes.Mean[source]¶

The arithmetic mean.

The arithmetic mean, or simly the mean or the average, is the first order moment of a probability denstiy function.

Examples

>>> stream = iter([1, 2, 3])
>>> for mean in Mean().yield_from(stream):
...   print(mean)
1.0
1.5
2.0
>>> mean = Mean()
>>> mean = mean.fit(1)
>>> print(mean.evaluate())
1.0
